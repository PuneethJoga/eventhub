Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for an event?

Simply browse our Events page, click on any event that interests you, and fill out the RSVP form on the event details page. You'll receive a confirmation email instantly.

Can I host my own event on EventHub?

Yes! Send us a message through the contact form above with your event details, and our team will get in touch to help you set it up.

Are there any cancellation fees?

Most of our events are free to attend. For paid events, cancellation policies vary and are mentioned on each event's details page.

How will I receive event updates?

Once you RSVP, you'll receive email updates about any changes to the event schedule, venue, or important announcements.
